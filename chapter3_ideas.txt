Villain: A shadowy sorcerer who feeds on forgotten memories, growing stronger as history fades from the minds of mortals.
Clue: Elara discovers a scorched parchment, half-burned but bearing an ancient rune — a fragment of a long-lost incantation.
Squeaky: A hyper-intelligent, time-traveling squirrel who speaks in riddles and helps Elara decode the Chronomancer’s Cipher.
